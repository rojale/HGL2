HGL Daily 1000 Series EU

The HGL Daily 1000 Series is a daily hearthstone tournament series that we host every night Monday through Friday at 7pm EET. The tournament is free to enter and has a prize of 1000 HGL Points, and you can use these points for awesome prizes including Hearthstone packs, gift cards, and even gaming gear such as headsets, keyboards, and other gaming gear! It is a single elimination tournament played in the conquest format. Early rounds are played in the Best-Of-3 conquest format up until quarter-finals where it then transitions to the Best-Of-5 conquest format. Players have an hour before the tournament to check-in and are required to join our Discord Server to connect with opposing players and speak with admins.

Tournament Information

  • Hosted Monday-Friday at 7 PM EET (Eastern European Time +2 UTC)
  • Check-In 1 hour before start time (6:00 EET)
  • EU Server
  • Free Entry

  • Bo3 Conquest Format - Bo5 Conquest Format for Quarters, Semis, and Finals (Click Here For Rules)
