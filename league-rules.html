Frequently Asked Questions

How do I sign up for a daily tournament?

Sign up for one of our daily Hearthstone tournaments by clicking here for America and here for Europe.

How can I earn HGL Points?

You can earn HGL Points through competing in our daily Hearthstone tournaments and finishing within the top 4 players.

What if I have the same amount of HGL Points as another player?

When players are tied for HGL Points, the player who earned the points first will get the higher placing on our standings.

Am I eligible to win prizes?

If you live within North America or select parts of Europe and Asia you are eligible for physical prizes. (Non-shipped prizes are digital and your location will not affect you receiving your prize)

What do I do if I'm not eligible to win a physical prize?

If you are not eligible to win a physical prize you will be rewarded a gift card of equal value.

How do I claim my prize at the end of the month?

At the end of the month when standings are finalized, players will be required to contact administration within 48 hours to redeem their prizes. HGL is not held responsible for the prizes that are not claimed within the 48 hour window
