Hearth Gaming League

The Hearth Gaming League is a monthly league that is 100% free to enter and rewards its top finishers with awesome prizes! We host daily Hearthstone tournaments for both America and Europe that reward players with HGL Points that will determine the monthly standings. At the end of each month the top standing players will be rewarded different prizes from keyboards and headsets to mice and gift cards!
